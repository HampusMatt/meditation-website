Here you can find 3 different types of meditations to try out!

Mindfullness

  • Sit down in a comfortable position. Read through the steps below, and start your journey into mindfullness:
  • Take a few minutes to just breathe. Watch your breath as your chest expands as you inhale, and colapse as you exhale.
  • Start to watch your own thoughts. Observe where your mind takes you.
  • Don't judge your mind if it starts racing, just become aware of the thoughts that pop up into your head.
  • Examine these thoughts, as if watching clouds in the sky from a distance, then let them pass as they please.
  • When you are ready, open your eyes and return to the room.

Body awareness

  • Sit down in a comfortable position. Read through the steps below, and start becomming more aware of your body.
  • Take a few minutes to just breathe. Watch your breath as your chest expands as you inhale, and colapse as you exhale.
  • Create some tension in your lower body; curl your toes, flex your ankles, and tighten your glutes. Hold for 10 seconds, then relax. Notice how you feel.
  • Create som tension in both your hands and arms; make a tight fist, flex your wrists and bend your elbows. Hold as hard as you can for 10 seconds. Then relax. Notice how you feel.
  • Create som tension in your neck and face; shrug your shoulders, and tighten all the muscles in your face. Hold for 10 seconds, then relax. Notice how your body feels.
  • Repeat these steps as many times as you like, then open your eyes and return to the room

Contemplation

  • Sit down in a comfortable position. Read through the steps below, and start contemplating the fleeting nature of existance.
  • Take a few moments to just breathe. Watch your breath as your chest expands as you inhale, and colapse as you exhale.
  • Imagine a stage, put all your material belongings on that stage. Your house, your clothes, all your little possesions you are fond of. Imagine them all there on that stage. Then let them dissapear.
  • Imagine all the people in your life. All your relatives, friends, coworkers and relations. Let them all step up onto that stage. One by one. See them all there, crowded and smiling. Then let them all dissapear away from you, never to appear again.
  • Imagine yourself stepping up onto that stage. There you are, with all your hopes and dreams, with all your values and beliefs, with all the experiences that makes you who you are. Then let yourself dissapear, fading away into nothingness.
  • Take a few moments to contemplate about the fleeting nature of existence, the fact that this is the path we all must tread. Then, when you are ready, open your eyes and return to the room.
