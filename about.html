About us

Who are we?

We are a couple of healthcare workers, with a wide array of expertise. We mainly work in hospitals, fighting diseases on a daily basis.

Mission statement

Our mission is to work with help promoting behaviours, specifically meditation. Health promotion is a behavioral social science that draws from the biological, environmental, psychological, physical and medical sciences to promote health and prevent disease, disability and premature death through education-driven voluntary behavior change activities. The result of our efforts is the website you see before you today.
